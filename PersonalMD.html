Description

The Lady Minutemen of Cocoa Beach Jr./Sr. High School consists of fourteen players, two captains, and two coaches.
The ladies compete with other teams in their district during their season, which runs from mid-October through the beginning of February.
The team competes at their home stadium at Cocoa Beach High and other team's home stadiums, like Satellite High or Westshore High.
The coaches of the team include the head JV coach, Chad Day, varsity coach, Fernando Terran, and assistant coach, Doug Monda.
