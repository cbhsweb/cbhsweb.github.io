WHAT IS STUDENT GOVERNMENT?

Student government is an entity that was created to provide students the experience and training in the democratic form of the government. This group of students is charged with the duty of continuously expanding and coordinating a program of student activities, to stimulate interest and support in the development, assessment and execution of educational, social, cultural and recreational programs, to develop communication amongst students, administration and faculty. In effect, to represent the best interests of the general student body .

MEETINGS

  • The CBHS Student Government association has biweekly meetings for both the middle school and high school associations at 8:00 a.m.
  • The Executive Board holds meetings when necessary, often to discuss topics like the incoming class officers or homecoming organization.
  • At both the middle and high school meetings the executive secretary is required to take minutes, every year before the states conference the historian organizes the minutes into a cohesive format along with summaries of the SGA events for each year into a Medallion Book.

    EVENTS AND PROGRAMS
    District 3 Meetings All schools that makeup the 3rd SGA district in the state of Florida meet several times each year to collectively discuss how to improve leaderships techniques and strategies along with how to excel in leading our schools.
    State Conference Each year all 6 student government districts send representatives from their schools to meet and bring to light issues in their individual districts, to discuss improvements being made, and to share ideas and methods they use to lead their schools
    Homecoming During the summer of every school year an executive board meeting is held where officers vote on a theme for the homecoming dance that fall, after a theme is chosen weekly meetings are held leading up to October to delegate preparation duties to members of each grade like selling tickets for the dance, decorating, ordering food, or organizing the homecoming game and parade respectively.
    Candy Grams Near Valentine's and Christmas SGA orders candy in bulk to sell to the students to be delivered to their friends in their classrooms.
