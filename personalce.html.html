Cocoa Beach Varsity Track Team

The Cocoa Beach Track Team , is a school team , that consists of students at the school who compete at daily track meets in running, throwing, and jumping events. The team consists of 9 th through 12 th graders, making it a Varsity team. There is a Junior Varsity (JV) team, that consists of 7 th and 8 th graders.

Practice

Practice for the Cocoa Beach Track Team is everyday after at 3:45.Practice if from 3:45 to 5:00 on mondays - fridays, except for wedensdays were they go from 2:30 to 4:00 . During spring break there are practices, they are led by the coaches. Practices before a meet are very short inorder to not tire out athletes before a race.
