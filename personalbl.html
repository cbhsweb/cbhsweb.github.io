Cocoa Beach's JV Softball Team practices at Cocoa Beach High School located on 1500 Minutemen Causeway, Cocoa Beach Florida. The athletics at Cocoa Beach is run by Fran Siljestrom, Email Fran Siljestrom
In order to tryout for the team you need to be attending Cocoa Beach Jr/Sr High School. More information about Cocoa Beach's JV Softball team will be discussed at meetings at the school. Information including tryout and conditioning dates will be discussed.
