Future Business Leaders of America ,or FBLA, helps students in Middle school and High school learn the necessary tools in order to start a career in the business world, and PBL, which is FBLA's college resource, continues on deployment in the business world.

FBLA offers a competitive environment for students to develop their skills, and for those who excel, they receive rewards and scholarships. It is run by Students for Students and has a hierarchy of leadership from the school to the district to the state and to the national level
