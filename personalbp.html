FBLA is the Future Business Leaders of America Club, a non-profit national organization for all Middle Level and

    WHY JOIN FBLA?
  • Participate in Regional competitions with CASH awards
  • Explore Leadership Opportunities
  • Apply for FBLA Scholarships
  • Get involved with community service fundraisers
  • It looks great on your college application!
    WHAT DO WE DO?
  • Community Service like the March of Dimes, Beach Cleanups, and Car Washes
  • Field Trips like FBLA District Rally
  • FBLA Florida State Leadership Conference in Orlando
  • Work with other FBLA Members and community leaders
  • Have fun together preparing presentations for competitions
    WHEN DO WE MEET?
  • Room 4-121 every other Thursday morning at 8am
  • Dues $20. Officers are elected by members - Treasurer, Historian, Reporter and Secretary.
