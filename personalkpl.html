What is Tropical Smoothie Cafe?
A Tropical-like Cafe where you and your friends can hang out while enjoying delicious Smoothies full of extraordinary ingredients. According to the companys website "Tropical Smoothie Cafe’s menu boasts bold, flavorful smoothies with a healthy appeal, all made-to-order from the freshest ingredients." The unique cafe also offers a variety of foods including: toasted wraps. sandwhiches, flatbreads and bowls. The cafe is an uncommon yet relaxing place where you can easily enjoy the company of others while simultatensouly enjoying some soul food.

Why I chose this
A great place for you and your friends to hang out and indulge in authentic food!! i enjoy coming here to eat and relax with my family and friends.


History of Tropical Smoothie Cafe
Tropical Smoothie Cafe originally started out on one of the many beaches in Florida. Once they started to becoming more popular they became a company in 1997 and opened their first official Cafe in Tallahasse,Florida in 1998. They started out with simple fresh ingredients and contnue to add more in their menu. Throughout the years tropical smoothie has expanded to over 630 cafes nationwide!
