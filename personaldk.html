Welcome to Cocoa Beach Literature Club!

About Us

A group of aspiring writers that meet once a week in order to discuss and learn about literature, as well as create our own works. We meet on the first and third Friday of school, after school.

The goal of the Literature Club is to improve the writing skills of our members.
