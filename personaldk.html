Welcome to Cocoa Beach Literature Club!

About Us

A group of aspiring writers and literary fans that meet once a week in order to discuss and learn about popular literature, as well as create our own works. We meet every Friday of school, after school.

The goal of the Literature Club is to improve the writing skills of our members and provide an artistic space for students to be able to write.

How it Works:

Most Members come to the literature club to learn how to improve their own writing skills, but a large part of the club involves reading other people's works and simply having time to read and write. Members have the options of writing, reading, showing other members what they have written, or simply discussing literature with others.

How to Join

Everyone is welcome to join! Stop by our meetings after school on Fridays or fill out an online form to join in on the fun.
