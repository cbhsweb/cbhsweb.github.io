Florida State Chapter

As the state organization of Future Business Leaders of America, Florida FBLA is dedicated to helping high school students improve their leadership skills and achieve academic goals. Florida FBLA encourages members to partake in multiple FBLA-PBL events and conferences across the state and nationally, enabling members to enhance their individual skills and gain monetary prizes, scholarship opportunities, and university connections. The first FBLA chapter was founded in 1948 at Hillsborough High School in Tampa and has since spread with over 15,000 members across Florida, making it one of the largest FBLA-PBL chapters in the country!
