About

The Cloud Appreciation Club is a club that tries to fully apreciate the wonder of the clouds.
We watch clouds as they roll by and try to learn about the clouds and why they exist, while appreciating the awe and beauty of them.
Every Thursday from after school until whenever you want to leave, we would watch the clouds, seeing how magestic they are.

Why

People are in this club because they love to appreciate nature. Clouds are very important for life, if they didn't exist we wouldn't either.
Because clouds are very important for all of us they should be appriciated, just like the rest of nature. We are here to fill that void.
