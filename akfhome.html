About Pink Robotics

Pink Robotics is a FIRST robotics team which spans 5 different schools in Brevard County, Florida. The team includes members from Cocoa Beach Jr./Sr. High School, Viera High School, Rockledge High School, Space Coast High School, and Satelite High School. The team also has many mentors from varying profesasions including NASA engineers as well as engineers for SpaceX, Lockheed Martin, and other private engineering organizations.

The goal of Pink Roboitics is to spread the ideas of STEM subjects and to introduce students to the engineering design process as well as allow them to gain experience from professionals in engineering fields. However, Pink Robotics also seeks to make this fun for students by competing against other schools and working together to achieve a goal which would be near impossible by themselves.
