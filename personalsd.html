NCT Dream is the 3rd sub-unit of the boy group NCT. The sub-unit currently consists of 7 members: Mark, Renjun, Jeno, Haechan, Chenle, Jisung and Jaemin. But NCT Dream is a rotational group, meaning that once a member turns 20 in Korea, they will be removed from the group and be placed into a different sub-unit, if they haven't already. NCT Dream debuted on August 24, 2016 under SM Entertainment.

    Except for Renjun and Chenle, all the members of NCT, including the members who aren't in Dream, were formerly part of SM Rookies, a pre-debut group of S.M. Entertainment trainees. SM Rookies were pretty popular, and they travelled to different countries to perform for their fans, which most groups didn't have pre-debut. Mark, Jeno, Haechan, Jaemin, and Jisung also went on different shows pre-debut, including EXO 902014, which followed popular K-pop group EXO in recreating music videos from the 90s, and The Mickey Mouse Club, which included them and four female SM Rookies as they play various games and learn lessons about being a K-pop idol.
