Students in Action was formed to to compete within the Jefferson Awards Foundation. Jefferson Awards Foundation

Description

Students in Action is a student led club who's mission statement is to help those struggling in the local community and abroad. We are focused on improving society and the community through volunteer service. Dedication to helping others is the key focus of the club; the club has been very sucessful at this task and has been awarded multiple times. Students in Action works directly with the Jefferson Awards Foundation and the regional advisor, Ms. Fidance. Our club advisor is Steve Primus, an administrator at Cocoa Beach Jr/Sr High School.

Projects for 2017-2018

  1. Secret Sanata-We gave presents away to students at our school that unfortunetly could not afford presents otherwise.
  2. Thanksgiving Food Drive-We raised donations of food and over 23 turkeys for struggling families at our school that could not afford to have a thanksgiving dinner.
  3. School Food Drive- Raised nonperishables for the local community.
  4. Clothing Drive-Raised clothing items for the community and students at our school.
