The Golden State Warriors



The Golden State warriors have been around since 1946. The Golden state warriors have changed locations a two times throughout the history of the franchise. They used to be the Philadelphia Warriors untill they changed to San Francisco. The Golden State Warriors have come a very long way team and are now undoubtly one of the best teams in the NBA



Owner: Joe Lacob & Peter Guber Joe Lacob is an American Buisiness executive who is currently a partner at Kleiner Perkins Caufield & Byers, Peter Guber is the chairman and and CEO of Mandalay Entertainment
General Manager: Robert Michael Myers
Head Coach: Steve Kerr
Arena: Oracle Arena The Arena opened in 1966
Franchise Value: $2.6 Billion 3rd overall (source:Forbes)
