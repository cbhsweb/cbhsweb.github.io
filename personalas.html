The New York Yankees have won the World Series 27 times, which is more than twice the number of wins of the second ranked team!
Many of their players were inducted into the Baseball Hall of Fame, and they continue to invest in some of the best players, including...


  1. Babe Ruth: Averaged 47 home runs per season
  2. Mickey Mantle: Averaged 40 home runs per year
  3. Lou Gehrig: 12 straight seasons of over 27 home runs
  4. Joe Dimaggio: 5 seasons with over 30 home runs
  5. Mariano Rivera: Has the lowest postseason ERA


Babe Ruth

History

The team was bought by Frank J. Farrell and William Stephen Devery.
At first, the team rarely made it to the championships, until Babe Ruth joined their team after their 1919 season.
With Ruth, they won their first AL title in 1921 and a World Series championship in 1923. They went on to set many records and win 27 World Series through the 1900s.
