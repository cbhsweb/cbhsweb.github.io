    The Patriots are a football team that have very good players like Tom Brady. They are so good that they lost to the Eagles just to see what losing feels like (If they tried they would have won). The Patriots have nice fans and they have good sportsmanship when they lose. The logo is a picture of a Minuteman, a n=man that could get ready for war in a minute.
