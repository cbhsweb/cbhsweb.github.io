About

In chorus, we learn different songs, perform both on stage and at festivals such as Music USA and get judged at MPA. We also go on field trips every year and have two yearly concerts here at CBHS. Other classes in the music program include Middle School Chorus, Band, Orchestra, and Chamber Orchestra.

Director

The choir director of CBHS High School and Middle School Concert Choir is Steven Murray. He has been at CBHS for 4 years, after being a substitute teacher. He has been a band director, orchestra director, and a church choir director. This year, the classes that his is teaching are middle school concert choir, high school concert choir, middle school concert orchestra, high school concert orchestra, and chamber orchestra. This year, the High School Concert Choir has gotten an overall excellent at MPA, and a 1st place Superior at the Music USA Festival at Universal Studios.
