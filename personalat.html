Coaches

Cocoa Beach offers volleyball at the varsity, junior varsity, and freshman level. The coaches at these level are:

  • Varsity-Coach Terrance
  • Junior Varsity-Coach Terrance
  • Freshman-Coach Sam

Summer Practices

The official seasons begins July 31, but over the summer the following programs are offered to returning players to get ready for the season:

  • Summer practices at the CBHS gym
  • Conditioning at the Cocoa Beach Pier
  • Pre-season scrimages between others schools in the county
