    Meets

    During season, meets occur every thursday. At the beginning of the seaso there is one relay meet which consists of just relays. The rest are all duel meets. The location depends on the season. It will take place at either of the two schools participating. Based off of personal experience, a majority occurs at our home pool. Meets at home start at around 4:00 and usually last until 6:00. It depends on how everything goes. Away meets vary. Usually they start later, around 4:30 and last until around 7:00.

      Some Meet Traditions
    • Every meet starts with the national anthem
    • One gender does land warm up wile the other warms up in the pool. Genders switch every week
    • Senior Night
    • After home meets, the team meets up at Coconuts on the Beach for dinner
    • Pasta dinner the night before CCC
    • During the CCC national anthem we have middle schoolers swim across the pool with kickboards and an American flag
    • After districts, the team goes to Golden Corral
