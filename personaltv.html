About There are 3 different cheer teams at Cocoa Beach High School. Middle schools cheer, JV cheer, and varisty. Cheerleaders do different cheers, jumps, dances, and on JV and varisity, stunt! There are many different types of jumps, pike, herky, toetouch, and double nine are just a few. When stunting we have 3 different positions, first base, second base, backspot, and flyer. There's also many different different types of stunts. We do prep, extension, swedish falls, twist up, lib, and many more! Our coaches make dances for us to learn and we preform them at pep ralleys and games.
