The Patriots are an NFL team with 5 Superbowl trophies. These trophies are not due to luck, but pure skill. Tom Brady is the leading quarterback and is a guaranteed NFL Hall of Famer. He is said to be the best quarterback the NFL has ever seen. Their 2nd star player is Rob Gronkowski, a tight end. Tom Brady and him make an amazing team and have shown their skill in many games before. With their star lineup, they are, by far, the best team in the NFL.
