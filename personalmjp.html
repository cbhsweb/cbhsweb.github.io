What do we do?

In this club, we watch and critique student speeches, discuss current and controversial topics, write and rehearse our own original speeches, and participate in speech and debate competitions!

    • Why join?

    There are Opportunities to Learn About Tons of Current Issues

    This club is a great place to get information about all the hottest topics in the news. Listening to speeches, researching topics, and debating with classmates will give your brain muscles a great workout, and you might learn something, too! Next time your relatives get political at the dinner table, you can impress them with all your knowledge of current events.

    It's Good Practice for a Lot of Important Skills

    Did you know public speaking is one of the most common fears? Well, between in-class debates, refining and critiquing speeches, and speaking in competition, I guaruntee you'll get a lot of practice and feel much more comfortable doing it. Additionally, your argument skills get a boost, and in the process of writing and researching for your speeches, your essay-writing skills are sure to get a major boost!

    You Get to Yell at People

    Did I mention you ge to debate in meetings? Who doesn't like arguing with people about political stuff... and it being encouraged!
